Explore Our Services

410 NEMT delivers trusted non-emergency transportation for Self-Pay, Medicaid, and insured clients. From routine checkups to specialist visits, we make it easy to get where you need to go—safely, comfortably, and on time.

Routine Medical Appointments

Safe, door-to-door transport to doctors, specialists, and outpatient clinics.

Post-Hospital & Rehab Discharges

Timely and secure transportation from hospitals, rehab centers, or urgent care facilities.

Senior & Assisted Living Transportation

Compassionate, door-to-door rides for elderly passengers needing support getting to and from medical facilities, pharmacies, and more.

Dialysis, Chemotherapy & Ongoing Treatments

Consistent, comfortable rides for patients receiving regular care.

Pediatric NEMT

Safe, family-accompanied rides for children with medical needs, including school-to-therapy transport.

Veterans’ Medical Transportation

Supportive services for veterans attending VA appointments or rehab programs.

Non-Medical Companion Transport

Transportation for errands, grocery trips, or social visits for individuals who require mobility support.

Long-Distance Medical Transport

Inter-county or out-of-town rides for clients needing specialized care beyond the local area.

After-Hours or Weekend Transport

Flexible scheduling for early morning, evening, or weekend medical appointments and discharges.

Hospital-to-Facility Transfers

Flexible scheduling for early morning, evening, or weekend medical appointments and discharges.
