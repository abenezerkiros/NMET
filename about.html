Reliable and Compassionate Transportation Services

At 410 NEMT , we believe transportation is more than getting from point A to point B — it’s about dignity, safety, and peace of mind.


Founded and guided by a licensed healthcare provider, our company was created to bridge the gap between medical care and reliable access. While we don’t provide clinical care during transport, our services are shaped by real healthcare insight — ensuring each ride is handled with care, professionalism, and attention to individual needs.


We specialize in non-emergency medical transportation for seniors, individuals with disabilities, patients recovering from treatment, and anyone who needs dependable support getting to and from healthcare services. From routine appointments to hospital discharges and therapy sessions, our trained drivers and wheelchair-accessible vehicles are ready to serve.


We’re proud to be a local, family-rooted business with a mission to uplift our community. Every trip we make is about more than just logistics — it’s about making life easier for our passengers and the people who care for them.


Reliable. Respectful. Clinically guided. That’s the 410 NEMT promise.

Why We’re Different

At 410 NEMT, we stand apart from standard transportation services by bringing healthcare-informed awareness to every ride. Our leadership includes a licensed healthcare professional, giving us a deep understanding of how important safe, timely, and respectful transport is—especially for individuals with medical appointments, mobility challenges, or post-treatment transitions.


While we do not administer medical care or services during transport, our operations are thoughtfully shaped by clinical insight. This means we prioritize clear communication, passenger safety, and comfort at every step. From how we train our team to how we structure our routes, that extra layer of understanding makes a difference.


Our drivers and support staff are trained not only in professional transportation protocols but also in delivering assistance with sensitivity and care. Whether we’re helping someone from their front door to a vehicle or coordinating a complex pickup, we treat every client with the dignity and attention they deserve.


We’re not just a ride—we’re a trusted bridge between care and community.
